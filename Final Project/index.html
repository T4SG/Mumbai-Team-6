Our Mission!

Happy Hearts Fund, founded in 2006 by Petra Nemcova after she survived the 2004 Indian Ocean Tsunami, rebuilds safe-resilient schools in areas impacted by natural disasters We work during the gap period when children are forgotten after emergency response is complete, bringing hope and empowerment to generations of children and entire communities.

Help us rebuild

At Happy Hearts Fund, your time, talent, work and heart go a long way! Every hour you spend as an HHF volunteer translates into the caring attention for a child that has survived a natural disaster. As a volunteer, you can assist with an HHF special project, help out in the office, help with “on the ground” projects or spread the mission of HHF in your community. Happy Hearts Fund needs more people like you… People with great hearts!

Schools that need your help

JOSE EMILIO PACHECO BERNY TELESECONDAY SCHOOL Coscomatepec, Veracruz Mexico Each year the Jose Emilio Pacheco Berny Telesecondary school is damaged by annual floods. This causes its 80 students to miss weeks of classes as the school recovers. The current structure is very old, unsafe and will continue to be flooded each year. This proactive and committed community is eager to send their children to a safer school. The community members have already worked with the Mexican government to secure a donation of land away from the flood zone and a donation of new furniture from the Minister of Education in Veracruz. Along with the generous support of Lexington Partners, all that is left to do for this community is to build a new school! With your help we can provide a new safe-resilient school for the Coscomatepec community!

Story of Change




The Head Teacher at TK Pringan Adji, Indonesia This new kindergarten is a great change especially for our children, who can now enjoy to learn in a comfortable place, they can improve their learning skills thanks to a variety of toys provided by donors. Also they really enjoy their nice and new playground, which help develop the children motor skills in a fun way. The biggest change is that now more and more parents want to send their children to TK Priangan Adji. They are confident and proud of our kindergarten. We are very proud of that and very grateful to Happy Hearts Fund.

Schools that need your help

DIVINO NINO JESUS SECONDARY SCHOOL
Chincha, Peru In August of 2007 a 8.0 magnitude earthquake struck Peru destroying the Divino Nino Jesus primary school in Chincha. Happy Hearts Fund, SURA and Lexington Partners jointly rebuilt the school in 2012. Since its reconstruction in 2012, the enrollment rate has grown over 50%, extensively exceeding capacity. Therefore the community built a temporary secondary school across from the primary school. This temporary structure is not in healthy condition and is not safe to withstand a future earthquake. With your help HHF will build a new safe-resilient secondary school with the capacity to host over 100 children for this proactive community in need.
